Various Weather Charactoristics

The goal of this webite is to show the relationship between weather patterns and latitude. It can be used to help predict various factors of weather given the latitude of the city. The factors of weather that we chose to analyze are Maximum temperature, cloudiness, and windspeed. The data used in this project comprises of over 500 cities from OpenWeatherMap API. After getting the data, we used Matplotlib to plot the results. This website shows maximum temperature, wind speed, humidity and cloudiness vary by latitude.
